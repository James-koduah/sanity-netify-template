Getting started with us

How do we begin work on my project?

To kick off our collaboration, the first step is to reach out - whether it's a call from you, an email, or we proactively contact you. During our initial meeting, we'll exchange greetings and delve into the specific services you're looking for.

After that, we'll schedule a video call, leveraging visual elements to gain a deeper understanding of your project. This allows us to discuss crucial details such as project scope, depth, timeline, and pricing, ensuring a comprehensive and successful partnership.

What communication channels do we use?

At Jaskin Tech, we prioritize high availability and transparency in our collaboration. Our primary communication methods include

  • Emails
  • Instant Messaging
  • Phone Calls
  • Video Calls
  • Social Media Platforms (If Applicable)

We leverage the most convenient of these methods to ensure effective communication throughout our partnership.

Will we sign a formal Contract?

In most cases, Yes. A contract serves as a formal agreement that outlines the expectations and responsibilities of both you, the client and us, the web agency. It can specify the scope of work, deliverables, timelines, and payment terms.

A well-drafted contract provides legal protection for both of us. It establishes a clear framework for our project, helping to mitigate potential disputes and misunderstandings. These contracts often address the ownership of intellectual property, ensuring clarity on who owns the rights to the final deliverables.

Read more on why a formal contract is essential.
How do I convey what I'm looking for and what I want?

Communicating your vision effectively is crucial. We recommend starting with a comprehensive discussion where you can share your goals, preferences, and any specific features you have in mind. Providing examples or references for websites you admire can also be helpful.

Additionally, we conduct a detailed requirements gathering session to ensure a thorough understanding of your needs. Throughout the process, we encourage open and clear communication to refine the vision and bring your ideas to life successfully.

Why are you so cheap?

We prioritize high-quality web solutions at competitive rates, achieved through efficiency and strategic resource management. Our transparent pricing ensures value, and we're open to tailoring services to meet your specific budget constraints.

Why are you so expensive?

Thank you for your inquiry. At Jaskin Tech, we aim for excellence. Our prices may be higher, but they guarantee top-notch expertise and results that last. Our value lies in the robust, long-term solutions we provide. We're open to discussing your needs and finding a tailored solution that aligns with your budget and goals.

Where are you located?

Although we are based in Ghana, we operate as a remote web agency, allowing our team to collaborate effectively from diverse locations. This remote approach enables us to deliver top-notch services while offering the flexibility to work with clients worldwide.
